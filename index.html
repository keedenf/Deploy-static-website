About me

For as long as I can remember I've always been curious about what goes on behind the screen, though I never had the opportunity to study software engineering growing up. I took it upon myself to find a way to learn despite having very little prior experience to programming. As with most people, once I found that one thing I enjoyed, I began to gravitate towards it through software engineering videos on youtube and conversations about programming with like-minded people. It was then when the spark was ignited.

I started working straight after my matric year and began saving in order to fund my studies through HyperionDev. Despite the adversities I faced along the way, working a full-time job and staying up late to push out assignments. I noticed that, that smothered flame never fizzled out. I realised that this was the path I was going to take and nothing could get in-between that. Since that realisation that flame has only burned brighter and I'm excited to see where my drive to further my knowledge takes me.
